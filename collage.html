Remix Digital Collage

Assignment Description

Create a new image from 3 or more image sources. Create a narrative that engages the audience. Consider how reuse of contect modifies, enhances, or negates aspects of the historical context of source material.

Process/Tools | Inspiration | Concept

Using Photoshop, I complied multiple images into a scene.
As a studio practice major, I don't have much experience working with Adobe applications. Photoshop was a new, and quite intimidating frontier for me.
As a recovering game addict, I thought it would be fun to do an RPG parody of "conquering" / "beating" the boss monster that is Photoshop.
