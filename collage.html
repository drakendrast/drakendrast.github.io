Remix Digital Collage


Assignment Description


Create a new composite image from 3 or more image sources. The image must communicate a narrative that engages the audience. Consider how reuse of contect modifies, enhances, or negates aspects of the historical context of source material.



Process/Tools | Inspiration | Concept


Using Photoshop, I complied multiple images into a scene.
As a Studio Practice major, my experience with the digital realm is rather lacking. Photoshop was a new, intimidating frontier for me.
Despite my lack of Adobe-savvy, I am a recovering game addict, and thought it would be fun to create an RPG parody of taking down the Boss-Level Monster I percieve Photoshop to be.
