Net Art


Assignment Description


Use whatever software necessary to create an Internet Artwork that offers a compelling experience (scope, interactivity, humor/engagement/twist, originality, clarity, usability/navigability) and takes advantage of the network in an interesting/provocative way. Project should capitalizing on the distinctive qualities that define the internet: hyperlinks and non-linear narrative.
"Write out some code that does something cool."



Process/Tools | Inspiration | Concept


I'm not sure why I decided to create something large like a visual novel. Usually visual novels take a lot of work, effort, and time - all three things I can't afford to give during finals crunch. My vision went from 6 worlds, to one world, to... just reaching the basement.
As a chronically indecisive person, I'm intensely familiar with the multitude of choices in real life. This game reflects my views on the power of "choice."



PLEASE NOTE: The story is incomplete.
The furthest in the game you can travel to is to the basement floor.

[SideNote]: Although I'm not exactly what python is, I think python might be a better platform for this type of game/story than html/css.
